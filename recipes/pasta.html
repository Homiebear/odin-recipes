Pasta

Description

A very simple recipe that anyone can make. Composde of rich marinara sauce and the most methodically shaped mostraccioli. This dish should take no longer than 15 to 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mostaccioli
  • 1/2 cup Ragu's Marina Sauce
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • Half an Onion
  • 1 tbsp Garlic
  • 1 tsp Oil

Steps

  1. Pour water in a pan
  2. Once water is boiling add mostaccioli
  3. Add salt
  4. After 15 minutes, drain water with colander
  5. Add oil to the pan
  6. Add onions
  7. Add garlic
  8. Add mostaccioli
  9. Stir for 1 minute
  10. Enjoy
