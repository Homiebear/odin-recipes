Ingredients

  • Garlic Bread
  • Ragu's Marina Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Put oil in the pan
  2. Pour Ragu's Marina Sauce in the pan
  3. Add onions
  4. Add salt
  5. Add garlic
  6. Pour sauce on garlic bread
  7. Bake garlic bread at 350 degrees F for 10 minutes
  8. Enjoy
